# Personal Contribution

Periodically review these criteria that will be used to evaluate your personal contribution at the end of each phase.

| ✓-  Below Standard | ✓  At Standard | ✓+  Above Standard |
| :---: | :---: | :---: |
| The team member did not consistently contribute to the team or was uncooperative at times. | The team member was consistently engaged and accountable for work needed, filling a valuable role for the team. | The team member often went above and beyond in the quality of personal work or adapted to needs of the team to complete work that was not initially planned. |
